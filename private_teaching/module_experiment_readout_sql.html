Part C: SQL Translation

Source

sql/cohort_m2_retention.sql

What it computes

1) base
Pulls only month 0 and month 2 rows from customer-month data.
2) cohort_denominator
Calculates month-0 gross baseline per family (denom_m0_gross).
3) m2_logo
Calculates M2 logo retention and month-0 customer counts.
4) m2_net
Calculates M2 net proxy retention with denominator guard.
5) final
Returns family metrics plus gap_pp for ranking.

Why this matters

It proves your metrics are not notebook-only. They can live in production SQL.

Your 3-Step Practice

  1. Pick one target family and fill the brief with concrete values.
  2. Fill the week-2 readout using mock deltas and a final decision.
  3. Walk through each SQL CTE and explain why it exists in one sentence.

Quick Quiz (Interview Prep)

Q1: Why define guardrails before launching?

Show answer

So a KPI lift does not hide economic damage (margin, credit-like shifts, or cohort quality loss).

Q2: Why is denominator guard needed for net proxy?

Show answer

If month-0 gross baseline is zero, the ratio is invalid. Guard prevents false infinite/undefined values.

Q3: Why separate brief and readout docs?

Show answer

The brief defines pre-test commitments; the readout evaluates outcomes against those commitments.
